The Evolution Of Technology

How it has changed the Word!!!

Since, the beginning of human civilization technology has played a significant role in the formation of the world as we know it today;
Beginning with the creation of relatively basic devices such as shadow clocks, sunstones, water clocks progessing to the development of very sophisticated systems such as digital clocks, mobile, computer, internet, and so on.

"The early days of technology"

Early age of technology determine by simple tools and their invention such as stone axes, shadow compass. These tools are used for simple task in our daily life such as cutting wood, breaking stone, finding the direction and so on. But it was taking 100% efficiency and giving only 50% of it efficiency to the work. Over changing time, civilizations developed and grew so did sophisticated tools and technology which enable to work as much as easier like made it easier for people to transport goods and travel on place to another place creating vehicle like car, bike, cycle, boat, plane etc. The invention of writing allowed people to record and share information leading to the development of early civilization and the creation of written records of human history.

The development of Metal working.

The development of metal working on the other hand allowed for the production of more durable and efficient tools and weapons leading to the improvement of tools such as transport vehicles, smart devices, daily life tools etc.

The agricultural Revolution

Agricultural Revolution made a major turning point in the evolution of technology. It was during this time that humans began to cultivate crops and raise lives stock for stable food supply and the development of settlements and civilizations.

Industrial Revolution

Late 18th to mid 19th century machine and factories were invented to the mass production of goods and shift from manual labor to machine based production. This revolution transformed the way goods were produced and distributed.
